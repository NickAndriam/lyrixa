## Features

### Lyrics at Your Fingertips

LyriXa provides a vast collection of lyrics from your favorite songs and artists. Access lyrics instantly to sing along or simply immerse yourself in the words behind the music.

### Seamless Music Playback

Enjoy your music seamlessly with LyriXa's built-in music player. Play your cherished tracks directly within the app, creating an effortless and immersive listening experience.

### Download for Offline Enjoyment

Take your music offline with LyriXa. Download your beloved songs and lyrics to enjoy them wherever you are, even without an internet connection.

### Discover Hidden Gems

Discover new artists, tracks, and musical gems that match your unique taste. LyriXa's recommendation system curates fresh tunes, ensuring you're always in sync with the latest music trends.

### Effortless Music Library Management

Effortlessly organize your music library within LyriXa. Create custom playlists, sort your tracks, and maintain your music collection with ease.

## Why LyriXa?

LyriXa is your go-to music companion, merging lyrics and music playback into a single, versatile app. Whether you're a lyrics enthusiast, a music lover, or simply in search of a reliable music player, LyriXa has you covered.
